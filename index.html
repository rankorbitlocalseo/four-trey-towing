What Are Roadside Assistance Services?

Roadside assistance refers to services provided to drivers when their vehicles experience minor mechanical issues. These issues usually don't require a tow but still leave the car immobile or unsafe to drive. Common examples include dead batteries, flat tires, lockouts, or running out of fuel. The goal of roadside assistance is to get your vehicle back on the road without needing to transport it.

What Is Emergency Towing?

On the other hand, emergency towing is specifically required when your vehicle cannot be repaired on-site or is unsafe to drive. This might happen due to a serious engine failure, collision damage, or mechanical problems that need professional repair in a shop. A towing service in Port Saint Lucie FL provides quick response and safe transport for such vehicles to a nearby mechanic or auto repair facility.
